前置詞（小テスト）

下の空欄に適した語（前置詞など）を入れてください。

  1. 1. She is tall ( ) Japanese.
    ※「日本人にしては背が高い」という意味
  2. 2. Make sure to submit this form ( ) two weeks.
  3. 3. Make sure to submit this form ( ) tomorrow.
  4. 4. He can fix anything ( ).
    ※「彼は何でも簡単に直せる」という意味
  5. 5. I can eat anything ( ) pineapples.
  6. 6. ( ) the summer vacation, all the books in BookOFF were on sale.
  7. 7. Bill Gates told us a key ( ) making a fortune.
  8. 8. The price of Tesla's shares dropped ( ) four thousand dollars.
  9. 9. I can't stop dancing ( ) the music.

模範解答（例）

  1. for a — She is tall for a Japanese.
  2. within — Make sure to submit this form within two weeks.
  3. by — Make sure to submit this form by tomorrow.
  4. with ease（または easily） — He can fix anything with ease.
  5. but — I can eat anything but pineapples.
  6. During — During the summer vacation, all the books in BookOFF were on sale.
  7. to — Bill Gates told us a key to making a fortune.
  8. by — The price of Tesla's shares dropped by four thousand dollars.
  9. to — I can't stop dancing to the music.
※7番を「a key to making a fortune」に修正済み。
